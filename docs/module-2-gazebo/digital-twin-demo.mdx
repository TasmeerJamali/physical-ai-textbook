## üß† How It Works

This simulation demonstrates **Forward Kinematics**: calculating the position of the "End Effector" (the gripper) based on the angles of the joints.

In **Module 1**, we learned the math behind this. In **Module 2**, we use Gazebo to simulate physics (gravity, collisions). Here, we use a lightweight web renderer to visualize the geometry.

### The Kinematic Chain

1.  **Base (J1)**: Rotates the entire arm around the vertical axis (Yaw).
2.  **Shoulder (J2)**: Lifts the main arm up and down (Pitch).
3.  **Elbow (J3)**: Extends or retracts the forearm (Pitch).
4.  **Wrist (J4)**: Rotates the gripper (Roll).
